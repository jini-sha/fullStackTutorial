Honey Pancakes Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 2 tablespoons honey (plus extra for topping)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, melted butter, and honey.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients. Do not overmix.
  4. Heat a pan and pour small scoops of batter.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook the other side.
  6. Serve warm with extra honey on top.
